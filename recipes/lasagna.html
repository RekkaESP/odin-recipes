Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the comforting layers of flavor with this classic homemade lasagna recipe. This timeless dish combines perfectly seasoned meat sauce, creamy béchamel, gooey melted cheese, and tender pasta for a meal that's both satisfying and delicious. Whether you're cooking for a family dinner or a special occasion, this lasagna is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, Italian sausage, chopped onion, and minced garlic until the meat is cooked through and the onion is translucent. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  3. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the egg, mixing well.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375˚F (190˚C)
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thing layer of the meat sauce. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  6. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with a generous layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot

Enjoy this homemade lasagna that captures the essence of traditional Italian comfort food!
