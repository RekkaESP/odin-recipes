Puchero

Embark on a culinary journey with Puchero, a hearty and flavorful stew that reflects the rich tradition of Spanish and Latin American cuisine. This comforting dish features a medley of meats, vegetables, and legumes, simmered to perfection in a savory broth.

Puchero is a celebration of diverse flavors coming together, creating a wholesome and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef brisket, cut into chunks
  • 1 pound chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 1/2 pound chorizo sausage, sliced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 ear of corn, cut into thirds
  • 1 medium-sized cabbage, quartered
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine beef brisket, chicken thighs, and chorizo. Fill the pot with enough water to cover the meats. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer and skim off any foam.
  2. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, diced tomatoes, carrots, and potatoes to the pot. Allow to simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Add soaked chickpeas, green beans, corn, and quartered cabbage to the pot. Season with bay leaf, paprika, salt, and pepper. Simmer for an additional 30-40 minutes until all ingredients are tender.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste and discard the bay leaf.
  5. Server the puchero hot, making sure to include a variety of meats, vegetables, and broth in each bowl.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Experience the heartwarming and diverse flavors of Puchero, a comforting stew that brings people together around the table.
